## Download Strategy

1. **Plain text format** - Easiest to parse
2. **One file per book** - Stored in `generation/books/`
3. **Metadata** - Title, author, year, chapter structure
4. **Chapter splitting** - For question generation by section

## Estimated Storage
- ~160 books
- ~50-100 MB total (plain text)
- ~500-1000 chapters for question generation
